Women in Tech

The We Cast team is back with Episode 2 and today, we’re having a conversation really close to our hearts: Women in Tech. It’s no exaggeration that women definitely have a different and greater set of challenges to face when entering any field, and that inequity is even more pronounced in the tech sector. Stereotypes, family pressures, societal norms, and a lack of support after hiring are just a few things that make them lose any hope of making it to the top. Today, we discuss how our community as a whole can do better by first addressing the situation, and then taking action. Join along and try to follow the advice so you too can be a part of the change!
